Gautam Jha

Gautam Jha is a highly respected and award-winning digital transformation leader with over two decades of experience in strategy, customer experience (CX), and digital transformation. He has helped high-profile companies like Marks and Spencer, TVH parts, Legal & General, Vodafone, Bank of New York Mellon, Telstra, and Avaya to successfully execute actionable CX-led digital strategies, solving their toughest business and technology challenges.

As a researcher at the esteemed Cambridge Service Alliance at the University of Cambridge, Gautam offers a novel CX-centric approach for organizations to achieve competitive differentiation and sustained loyalty. He serves on transformation boards, advising senior executives with his unique blend of practice-based experience, research-based knowledge, and technology background.
